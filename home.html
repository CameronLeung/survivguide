Background  on  Surviv.io

Surviv.io is a popular 2d battle royale game in which players spawn on an island and gear up to fight each other to be the last one standing. Players must choose from a wide variety of different weapons, skins, and equipment in order to outplay their opponents and survive. Players in Surviv.io can even play with their friends in duo or squad modes.
