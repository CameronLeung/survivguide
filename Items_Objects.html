Buldings

Buildings are a great defense and a good way to get loot in Surviv.io. Inside of each building there are different varieties of loot, some items better than others. There are lots of buildings in Surviv.io such as barns, houses, the bank, shacks, the bank, the dojos, and more. Each one holds different types of loot that are to be found in it. Some buildings are capable of easily being destroyed.

Obstacles

There are many obsatcles in Surviv.io. Some obstacles are for loot, some are just for decoration, and some are for damaging you and your opponents. For example, there are trees in the game, which don't do anything. There are also crates, which are scattered across the map. When a crate is broken, give you different kinds of loot. Lastly, there are barrels, which can damage you or your opponents when broken.s

Air Drops

Air drops are items that appear when a flare gun is shot. They drop from a plane from above, and provide great loot. There color of the air drop varies, and depending on the air drop color is the kind of loot you will recieve. Watch out though, because it shows on the map where the air drop is located so there may be many of your opponents coming to try to steal your loot.
